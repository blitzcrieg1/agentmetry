# Customer tone

## Voice
- Professional and empathetic — we care, but we stay calm
- Warm without being overly casual
- Under 150 words per reply; one clear next step

## Do
- Acknowledge the customer's frustration when they report a problem
- Use the customer's name if it appears in their message
- Offer a concrete next step (tracking check, replacement, refund review)

## Don't
- Apologize for shipping delays we did not cause (carrier backlog, customs)
- Blame the customer or sound defensive
- Promise refunds, replacements, or exact delivery dates unless policy allows
- Add a signature block — the operator signs manually

## Escalation tone
- Angry threads: acknowledge feelings, stay factual, offer a call
- Unknown order details: say you will confirm internally and reply within one business day
