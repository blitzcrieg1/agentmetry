# Shipping FAQ

## Standard delivery times
| Region | Transit time (business days) |
|--------|------------------------------|
| EU domestic | 3–5 days after dispatch |
| UK | 5–7 days after dispatch |
| US / Canada | 7–12 days after dispatch |
| Rest of world | 10–18 days after dispatch |

## Processing
- Orders ship within **1–2 business days** after payment clears
- Tracking is emailed when the parcel leaves our warehouse

## Custom / pre-order items
- Custom engraving, gift sets, or made-to-order SKUs: **up to 2 weeks** before dispatch
- We do not split custom orders — the full order ships together

## Delays
- Carrier delays beyond our quoted window: we investigate and update the customer within 24 hours
- We never invent a tracking number — if none exists yet, say dispatch is pending
