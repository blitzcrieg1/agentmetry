# Returns policy

## Window
- **14 days** from delivery to request a return
- Item must be unused, in original packaging, with proof of purchase

## Exclusions
- Opened skincare / hygiene products (unless defective on arrival)
- Custom or personalized items
- Final-sale clearance items (marked at checkout)

## Process
1. Customer emails support with order number and reason
2. We send a return label (EU) or return instructions (international) within 2 business days
3. Refund issued within **5 business days** after we receive and inspect the item

## Defective or wrong item
- Full refund or replacement at our cost — no return shipping fee
- Photo of damage required before we authorize a replacement

## What not to promise in email
- Refund before inspection
- Return windows longer than 14 days without manager approval
