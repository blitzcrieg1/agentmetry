# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Tone
- **Technical peer** for engineering leaders — never salesy or hype-driven
- **Confident but humble** — we know our space, we don't oversell
- **Local-first** is our key differentiator — emphasize privacy, control, offline capability

## Do
- Reference specific recent events (funding, product launches, blog posts)
- Keep emails under 150 words
- Lead with value, not features

## Don't
- Use "revolutionary", "game-changing", or similar empty superlatives
- Send generic templates without personalization
- Mention competitors by name negatively
