# Agentmetry Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

**Version 1.0** · Effective 2026-07-16 · Pairs with [CLA.md](CLA.md) (ICLA v1.0)

### Plain-language summary

This agreement is for **companies** whose employees contribute to Agentmetry.
The company grants **blitzcrieg1** (the Project Owner) the same rights described
in the Individual CLA — including the right to use contributions in the open-source
project and to **relicense them** under other terms (including commercial licenses).

---

This Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") is between the entity
named below ("Corporation") and blitzcrieg1 ("Project Owner") for contributions
to Agentmetry ("the Project").

1. **Definitions.** "Contribution" means any original work of authorship,
   including modifications or additions to existing work, that an employee or
   contractor of Corporation submits to the Project with Corporation's authorization.

2. **Copyright and Patent Grants.** Corporation grants to the Project Owner the
   same copyright and patent licenses described in [CLA.md](CLA.md) sections 2
   and 3, for all Contributions made by its authorized personnel.

3. **Right to Relicense.** Corporation grants to the Project Owner the same
   right to relicense Contributions described in [CLA.md](CLA.md) section 4.

4. **Authorization.** Corporation represents that it has authorized the listed
   contributors to submit work under this Agreement and that Corporation has the
   legal authority to enter into this Agreement.

5. **Contact.** To execute this CCLA, email **legal@agentmetry.ai** with:
   - Legal entity name and jurisdiction
   - Authorized signatory name and title
   - GitHub organization or usernames of contributors covered
   - Confirmation of agreement to CCLA v1.0

Signed CCLAs may be recorded in `signatures/corporate/` at the Project Owner's
discretion.
